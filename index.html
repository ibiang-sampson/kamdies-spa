Our Story

Redefining Beauty

Kamdies Skincare and Spa is a premier beauty and wellness destination located in Enugu and Asaba, Nigeria. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to providing top-tier skincare solutions and luxurious spa treatments designed to rejuvenate your body and enhance your natural glow.

We believe that beauty is not just about appearance; it's about confidence and wellness. Our holistic approach combines advanced skincare science with traditional spa therapies to deliver results that you can see and feel.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of holistic beauty and wellness solutions in Nigeria, empowering individuals to look and feel their best.

Chichi Gift Okwor Egbule

Founder & Lead Esthetician

Meet The Founder

Kamdies Skincare Spa was founded by Chichi Gift Okwor Egbule, a renowned Nigerian entrepreneur, licensed esthetician, and former Nollywood actor. Hailing from Delta State, Chichi combines her passion for beauty with professional expertise to deliver results-driven skincare treatments.

Her transition from the screen to the spa industry was driven by a deep desire to help people solve their skin concerns and find relaxation in a chaotic world. Today, she leads a team of certified professionals who share her commitment to excellence.
